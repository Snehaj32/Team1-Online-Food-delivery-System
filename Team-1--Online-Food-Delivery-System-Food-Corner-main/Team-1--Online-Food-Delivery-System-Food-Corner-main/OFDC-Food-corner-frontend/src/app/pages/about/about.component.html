Food Corner is a website designated primarily for use in the food delivery industry. This system allows to quickly and easily Page 3 of 19 manage an online menu which customers can browse and use to place orders with just few clicks. The Online Food Delivery System that we are proposing here, greatly simplifies the ordering and delivering process for both the customer and the restaurant. The system presents an interactive and up-to-date menu with all available options in an easy to use manner. Customer can choose one or more items to place an order which will land in the Cart. Customer can view all the order details in the cart before checking out. At the end, customer gets order confirmation details. Users also get the facility of tracing their order whenever they want.
